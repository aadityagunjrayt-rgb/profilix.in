This Privacy Policy explains in plain language what information we collect when you use our services, how we use it, and the rights you have over it. We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us for the purpose of building your profile website. This includes:

  • Your full name — as you want it displayed on your profile page
  • Profile photo — the image you share with us to be shown on your profile
  • Professional role or tagline — a short description of what you do
  • Bio or about text — a brief introduction about yourself that you write or ask us to help with

We do not collect any data automatically, use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or sell your information to any third party — ever. Profilix is ad-free and always will be.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to create, maintain, and improve your personal profile website. Specifically, we use it to:

Data Storage & Security

Your profile data is stored on secure servers. We follow industry-standard practices to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. While no system is 100% immune to breaches, we take every reasonable measure to keep your data safe.

We retain your data for as long as your profile subscription is active. If you choose not to renew and your grace period expires, we may delete your profile data after a reasonable period. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

  • Correction — Ask us to correct any inaccurate information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at profilix.in@gmail.com or WhatsApp +91 62071 26785. We respond within 48 hours.

After your profile is live, you can request content updates as follows:
