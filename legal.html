Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Profilix to create your personal website, we only collect the information necessary to provide and customize our services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Your full name, email address, WhatsApp number, and chosen username.
  • Profile Data: Photos, biographical text, social media links, and any portfolio/resume files you choose to upload to your profile.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics regarding how your profile is accessed, helping us ensure fast load times and service stability.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is exclusively used for the setup, maintenance, and hosting of your unique Profilix website. We use your contact information solely to communicate updates regarding your account, renewal reminders, and support queries.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not inject third-party trackers or ads onto your personal profile. Your profile is public by nature, so the information you ask us to put on your page will be visible to anyone with your link.

4. Your Rights & Data Removal

You own your content. If you wish to take down your website, update your details, or completely erase your data from our servers, simply contact our support team via WhatsApp or email, and we will process the deletion within 48 hours.

Usernames (e.g., profilix.in/yourname) are distributed on a strict first-come, first-served basis. Reserving a username requires active payment of a subscription plan. If your plan expires and the grace period passes, the username may be released to the public.

You retain full ownership of the text, images, and files you provide to us. However, Profilix retains all ownership and intellectual property rights over the custom design templates, underlying code, layouts, and overall UI/UX of the platform.

Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. One-Time Setup Fee

The initial setup fee covers the manual labor and technical resources required to design and deploy your custom profile within 48 hours. Because this is a personalized digital service, the setup fee is non-refundable once development has begun.

2. Satisfaction Guarantee

We want you to love your profile. If you are not satisfied with the initial delivery, we offer revisions to tweak the design, colors, and layout until it meets the standards of your chosen template. If we completely fail to deliver the website as promised, a full refund will be issued.

3. Subscription Cancellations

You can choose not to renew your 4-month or 12-month subscription at any time. There are no lock-in contracts or cancellation fees. However, we do not offer prorated refunds for mid-cycle cancellations. Your profile will remain active until the end of your currently paid billing cycle.

Service Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Delivery Timeline

Our standard delivery promise is 48 hours from the moment we receive all your required information (photos, text, links) and payment confirmation. If there are delays on our end, you will be notified immediately.

2. Profile Updates

The frequency of content updates (changing a photo, updating a link, adding a new resume) depends on your plan:

  • Setup Plan: No recurring free updates (updates incur a small fee).
  • 4-Month Plan: 1 free update per month.
  • 12-Month Plan: Unlimited free updates.

Update requests are typically processed within 24-48 hours via WhatsApp support.

3. Uptime & Maintenance

We strive for a 99.9% uptime for all user profiles. Occasionally, short maintenance windows are required to upgrade our infrastructure. These are generally performed during off-peak hours to minimize disruption to your digital presence.
